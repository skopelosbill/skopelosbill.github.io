About the Owners

Jan and Howard

Welcome to our website, we hope you enjoy looking over it and we look forward to welcoming you to our beautiful Greek Island town house soon so that you can experience and enjoy life on Skopelos. We have lived on Skopelos since 2020 in a farmhouse on a small olive farm with our dogs. We love Skopelos and the simple way of life here which you will experience first hand during your stay at Miro Cottage.
